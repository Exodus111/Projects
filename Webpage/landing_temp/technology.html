Groupthink AI

Groupthink is a new AI technology, invented in house. That handles game AI in a new fashion by focusing on group behavior, instead of individual states. The Enemies the player face will cooperate, they will strategize, and they will help each other. This presents the player which a much more interesting and varied experience when facing against groups of Enemies.

Top Down Procedural Generation

Procedural Generation allows for endless variety of game content, created by Computer algorithm. A top down approach to this technology, removes the constraints of randomness and sameness, as it creates content based on its narrative value, not just a random dice roll.

What this means is that, in the case of a World Generator, instead a random map with towns and cities strewn helter skelter around. A choice is first made as to what this world is all about, and what is going on. Is it a dark world ruled by an evil overlord. Is it 2 kingdoms locked in perpetual warfare. Are Old Gods awakening in an otherwise peaceful land. This narrative choice is made first, and the world is generated in accordance with that directive. To use another example, when procedurally generating a house, instead of just generating rooms and furniture, we first decice who lives there. A criminal. A member of high standing in society. A single mother or 5 brothers living in cramped quarters. With that choice out of the way the house is generated accordingly.

A Top Down approach is technically more demanding, but is a far more interesting gameplay experience for the player.
